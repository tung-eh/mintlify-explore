# **Add employees to your business account**

Before using Camelo to manage shifts and connect with your team, you need to [<u>add members to your business account</u>](https://help.camelohq.com/articles/guide/scheduling/employees-and-members/add-employees-to-business-account/).

There are 4 ways you can add employees:

1. [<u>Enter emails manually</u>](https://help.camelohq.com/articles/guide/scheduling/employees-and-members/add-employees-to-business-account/#1-add-emails-manually)
2. [<u>Share a join code</u>](https://help.camelohq.com/articles/guide/scheduling/employees-and-members/add-employees-to-business-account/#2-share-a-secret-join-code)
3. [<u>Import from phone contacts</u>](https://help.camelohq.com/articles/guide/scheduling/employees-and-members/add-employees/#3-import-employees-from-contacts)
4. [<u>Import employees from spreadsheet file</u>](https://help.camelohq.com/articles/guide/scheduling/employees-and-members/add-employees-to-business-account/#4-import-employees-from-a-spreadsheet-file)
